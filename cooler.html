How It Works

Advanced liquid nitrogen technology for superior cooling performance

1

LN2 Tank Connection

User buys or rents a small Liquid Nitrogen (LN2) tank that connects securely to the HMI Cooler unit.

2

Cooling Process

LN2 flows through copper pipes inside the unit. Copper absorbs the cold (−196°C) and becomes extremely chilled.

3

Air Circulation

Fans blow air across the chilled copper pipes. Air instantly cools and is pushed into the room.

4

Energy Flexibility

Runs on grid power, but also supports battery backup and solar panel power for uninterrupted cooling.
